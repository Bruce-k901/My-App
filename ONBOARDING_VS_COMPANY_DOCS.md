## Why The Separation?

### Onboarding Documents
- **Employee-specific** - Each new hire gets a personalized pack
- **Role-based** - Different packs for FOH/BOH, Hourly/Salaried
- **Tracked** - Monitor which employees have acknowledged each document
- **Templated** - Same documents for all new starters

### Company Documents
- **Company-wide** - Not tied to individual employees
- **Reference** - Available for audits, inspections, day-to-day operations
- **Compliance** - Legal requirements, certifications, policies
- **Versioned** - Keep historical versions for audit trails
