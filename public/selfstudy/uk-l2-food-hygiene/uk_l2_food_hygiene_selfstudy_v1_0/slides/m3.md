## Cross-contamination routes

- Hands and gloves that touch raw then ready-to-eat foods
- Shared equipment without proper cleaning
- Splashing or dripping from raw storage

## Allergen essentials

- Know the 14 major allergens listed in UK law
- Keep recipe cards and supplier specs up to date
- Record every menu change in the allergen matrix immediately

## Controls that work

1. Prep allergen-free orders in a dedicated, clean space
2. Use single-use utensils or sanitised tools for allergen orders
3. Label and segregate allergens in storage and on the pass

## Service focus

- [ ] Check the allergen menu is current and accessible to staff
- [ ] Confirm coloured allergen tongs and boards are in place
- [ ] Brief front-of-house on how to respond to allergen questions
