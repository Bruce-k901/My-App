# Module 1 · Introduction and Legal Duties

## Why this matters

- Food businesses must follow UK food safety law at all times
- Inspectors from the local authority can arrive without warning
- Strong training and records protect your reputation and rating

## Key responsibilities

- **Food handlers**: follow procedures, keep training up to date, report issues
- **Supervisors**: coach staff, monitor standards, keep evidence of actions
- **Food business operator**: provide resources, maintain the Food Safety Management System (FSMS)

## What good looks like

1. Everyone knows their role in food safety
2. Records are accurate, current and easy to access
3. Corrective actions are logged and completed

- [ ] Open your Training Matrix and confirm onboarding is complete
