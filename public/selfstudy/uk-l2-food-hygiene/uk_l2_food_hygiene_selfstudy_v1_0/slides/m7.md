# Module 7 Â· Premises and Equipment

## Layout essentials

- Maintain clear separation between raw, cook and ready-to-eat zones
- Provide sufficient handwash stations with visual prompts
- Use smooth, cleanable surfaces and repair damage promptly

## Equipment care

- Follow manufacturer cleaning schedules for complex kit
- Calibrate probes and digital thermometers regularly
- Tag out faulty equipment and log maintenance calls

## Fabric checks

- Ceilings: keep free from peeling paint, leaks or mould
- Walls & floors: repair cracks, seal joints, maintain skirting
- Lighting: fit shatterproof covers and clean diffusers

## Monitoring ideas

- [ ] Complete weekly premises walkaround with a fresh pair of eyes
- [ ] Photograph issues and attach to maintenance tickets
- [ ] Review contractor reports for outstanding actions
