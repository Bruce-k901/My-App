# Module 4 Â· Personal Hygiene

## Golden rules

- Wash hands with hot water, antibacterial soap and paper towels
- Wear clean protective clothing, hair covering and minimal jewellery
- Cover cuts with blue waterproof dressings before starting work

## Illness policy

- Report stomach illness immediately; stay off food for 48 hours after symptoms
- Supervisors must record illness reports and decide fitness to work
- Visitors should sign in and confirm they are symptom free

## Daily habits

1. Handwashing on arrival, after breaks, after handling waste, after raw food
2. No eating, drinking or smoking in food prep areas
3. Keep nails short and unpainted; remove watches and bracelets

## Quick checks

- [ ] Verify handwash stations are stocked with soap and towels
- [ ] Replace any worn aprons or PPE before service
- [ ] Remind the team about the 48-hour exclusion rule
